# WorkMultiple - Brand Reference

> **Quick-reference brand guide for agents and collaborators.** For the full brand strategy, see `01 - Brand/Brand Foundation.md`.

---

## Identity

**Name:** WorkMultiple (one word, capital W, capital M)
**Domain:** workmultiple.com
**Tagline:** More done. Same team.
**Secondary tagline:** Multiply your workforce. (for contextual use on site, not primary)

---

## Voice

**Personality:** Capable. Clear. Grounded.

**We sound like:** A sharp operator who gets things done. A trusted contractor you'd rehire. Confident, not arrogant.

**We don't sound like:** A futurist giving a TED talk. A SaaS company selling seats. A hype machine. Salesy or desperate.

### Principles

1. **Lead with outcomes, not technology.** We sell time back, output gained, headcount saved. AI is the how, not the what.
2. **Be direct.** No fluff. Say what we do, what it costs, what they'll get.
3. **Make AI feel normal.** Practical tool, not a threat. No jargon.
4. **Show, don't tell.** We run our own business on the same tools we deploy for clients.

### Do / Don't Examples

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| "We turned a 6-hour weekly reporting process into a 10-minute automated briefing." | "We leverage cutting-edge agentic AI frameworks to optimize operational throughput." |
| "We'll audit your workflows, build the agents, and have them running in two weeks." | "We partner with forward-thinking organizations to co-create transformative digital experiences." |
| "Think of it like hiring a tireless assistant who never drops the ball." | "Our autonomous agents leverage large language models to execute multi-step reasoning chains." |

---

## Copy Rules

- **No em dashes** in any public-facing content (website, marketing, outreach, proposals).
- **No jargon.** Avoid: agentic frameworks, LLMs, multi-step reasoning, RAG, embeddings, autonomous systems. Use plain language.
- **Outcomes first.** Every piece of copy should answer "what does the reader get?" before explaining how.

## Visual Direction

**Theme:** Light (clean, approachable)
**Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
**Accent color:** `#e9594a` (coral/warm red)
**Accent hover:** `#d14d3f`
**Background:** `#FAFAFA`
**Surface:** `#FFFFFF`
**Text:** `#111111`
**Text secondary:** `#555555`
**Border:** `#E5E5E5`

**Aesthetic:** Stripe, Linear, Vercel. Confident and minimal. Whitespace, typography, subtle geometric elements. No stock photography. No gradients or heavy decoration.

**Logo:** Text-based wordmark for now ("WorkMultiple"). Slash treatment (Work/Multiple) under consideration but not finalized.
