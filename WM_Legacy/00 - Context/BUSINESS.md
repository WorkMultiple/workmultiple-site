## Target Audience

**Niche:** High-ticket specialized contractors. Custom home builders, high-end remodelers (kitchen, bath, whole-home), and commercial HVAC companies.

**Company size:** 2 to 25 employees.
**Sweet spot:** 5 to 15 employees. Large enough to have real operational pain (an overwhelmed office manager or project coordinator), small enough that they cannot justify hiring dedicated ops or tech staff.

**Revenue range:** $1M to $15M annually.

**Who we sell to:**
- **At companies with 2-10 people:** The owner or founder. They're doing everything and feeling it.
- **At companies with 10-25 people:** The office manager, project coordinator, or operations lead. The person actually drowning in the manual work. They champion the solution internally.

**What these people care about:** Fewer dropped leads, less time on the phone coordinating subs, clients who feel informed without the team having to manually update them, and getting their evenings back. They do NOT care about the underlying technology. They care about what it does for them.

**Why this niche:**
- High-ticket projects ($50K-$500K+) mean the budget exists and the ROI is immediate. One recovered lead pays for the entire engagement.
- Long project timelines (months, not days) create complex coordination workflows with high automation potential.
- The industry is massive ($500B+ US remodeling market), highly fragmented, and almost completely untouched by AI workflow automation.
- These buyers are not tech-forward. They use Buildertrend or CoConstruct for project management but do everything else manually. They will not build automation internally.
- Existing tools (ServiceTitan, Buildertrend, Procore) handle scheduling and project tracking. Nobody is automating lead follow-up, client communication sequences, subcontractor coordination, change order workflows, or post-project review campaigns. That is the gap.

## Services

We deploy AI agents for the specific workflows that eat up contractor time. Every workflow we build for clients, we've tested on ourselves first.

| Workflow | What it does |
|---|---|
| Lead Follow-Up and Qualification | Respond to inquiries, qualify leads, schedule consultations. No more leads going cold because the team was on a job site. |
| Client Updates During Projects | Automated progress reports and milestone notifications. Clients feel informed without the team sending manual emails every week. |
| Subcontractor Coordination | Scheduling reminders, document collection, payment tracking. The agent keeps subs on track so the project coordinator doesn't have to chase them. |
| Change Order Management | Capture changes, document scope, communicate updates. Reduces confusion and protects margins. |
| Post-Project Follow-Up | Review requests, warranty registration, referral solicitation. Turns completed projects into new business. |
| Estimate and Proposal Assembly | Pull from templates, customize per project, send to prospects. Faster turnaround on bids. |
