# WorkMultiple - Outreach Templates

> **Reusable outreach templates for cold email, LinkedIn, and follow-ups.** Every template follows the copy rules in `BRAND.md`. Personalize the placeholder fields before sending. These are starting points, not scripts.

---

## Copy Rules (Non-Negotiable)

Before using any template, review these rules. If the final message violates any of them, rewrite it.

1. No em dashes. Use commas, periods, or restructure.
2. No jargon. Never use: agentic frameworks, LLMs, RAG, embeddings, autonomous systems.
3. Outcomes first. The first sentence answers "what's in it for me?"
4. Be direct. Short sentences. No filler.
5. Make AI feel normal. Practical tool, not futuristic technology.
6. Don't sound salesy. No "just checking in," no "I'd love to pick your brain."

---

## Placeholder Key

Use these placeholders. Replace all of them before sending.

- `[First Name]` - Prospect's first name
- `[Company]` - Prospect's company name
- `[Industry-Specific Pain Point]` - A pain point from the ICP relevant to their industry
- `[Specific Detail]` - Something specific to the prospect (recent news, job posting, LinkedIn post, company milestone)
- `[Outcome Example]` - A concrete result or example relevant to their situation

## Personalization Guide

When filling in placeholders, use real information:

**[Specific Detail] examples by source:**
- Job posting: "I noticed [Company] is hiring for an operations coordinator"
- Growth signal: "[Company] just made the Inc 5000 list"
- Funding: "[Company] recently closed a Series A"
- LinkedIn post: "Saw your post about scaling the team this quarter"
- Portfolio growth: "[Company] has been expanding across the Salt Lake market"

**[Industry-Specific Pain Point] examples:**
- Agencies: "client reporting and campaign tracking"
- Accounting: "document prep, client onboarding, and recurring deliverables"
- E-commerce: "order reporting, marketing performance tracking, and support triage"
- Real estate: "tenant communications, maintenance requests, and weekly property reports"
- SaaS: "internal reporting, customer onboarding, and sales pipeline management"

**[Outcome Example] examples:**
- "Cut weekly reporting from 6 hours to 10 minutes"
- "Automated the client onboarding process, saving 8 hours per new client"
- "Built a lead research agent that qualifies prospects automatically"
- "Deployed agents that handle 80% of tenant communications without manual intervention"

---

## Sending Cadence

Recommended outreach sequence per prospect:

1. **Day 1:** Cold email (Template 1)
2. **Day 1-2:** LinkedIn connection request (Template 2)
3. **Day 4-5:** Follow-up email (Template 3)
4. **After LinkedIn connection accepted:** LinkedIn follow-up message (Template 4)
5. **Day 10-12:** Final follow-up email (Template 3, Variant B), then move on

Do not send more than two follow-ups. If there's no response after the sequence, move the prospect to a "nurture" list for revisiting in 60-90 days.
