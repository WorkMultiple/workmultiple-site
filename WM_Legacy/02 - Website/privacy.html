Privacy Policy

Effective February 27, 2026

WorkMultiple ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We keep this straightforward because that is how we operate.

What We Collect

When you book a discovery call through our website, our Google Calendar booking form collects:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your company website
  • Your answer to the question "What's taking your team the most time right now?"

We may also collect your name and email address if you contact us directly via email.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Schedule and conduct discovery calls with you
  • Understand your business needs so we can prepare for our conversation
  • Follow up with you after our call
  • Communicate with you about our services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Tools and Services We Use

To run our business and communicate with you, we use the following third-party services:

  • Google Workspace (including Gmail and Google Calendar) for email communication and scheduling. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed through their services.
  • Google Meet for video calls during discovery sessions. Calls are not recorded unless we ask for and receive your permission beforehand.
  • Netlify for hosting this website. Netlify may collect standard server logs (such as IP addresses and browser type) as part of normal website hosting.
  • Google Analytics for understanding how visitors use our website. This collects anonymized usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location. No personally identifiable information is collected through Analytics.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies from Google Analytics to understand site traffic and usage patterns. These are standard analytics cookies and do not track you across other websites. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored within Google Workspace, which provides enterprise-grade security including encryption in transit and at rest. We do not store your personal data in any other systems beyond what is necessary to communicate with you and deliver our services.

Your Rights

You can request to see, update, or delete any personal information we hold about you at any time. Just email us and we will take care of it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of the page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, reach out to us at emilio@workmultiple.com.
