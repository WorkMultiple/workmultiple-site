# Affiliate Program Signup Guide

Quick reference for applying to the three easy-approval affiliate networks. These accept most sites with original content. Apply now, get approved while content builds, then retrofit links into articles.

---

## 1. Amazon Associates

**Signup URL:** https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/signup

**What you need:**
- Your name, address, phone number
- workmultiple.com as your website URL
- Tax ID / SSN
- Bank account for payments
- Brief description of your site and how you'll use affiliate links

**Approval timeline:** Near-instant provisional approval. You get 180 days to make 3 qualifying sales, then they do a full review.

**Commission:** 1-10% depending on product category. Books/Kindle: 4.5%. Software: 2.5%.

**Why apply:** Products, books, and equipment referenced in articles. Low barrier, fast approval.

---

## 2. Impact.com (Publisher/Partner Account)

**Signup URL:** https://app.impact.com/signup/publisher

**What you need:**
- Email, name, website URL
- Brief description of your content and audience
- Verification via email link

**Approval timeline:** Account creation is instant. Individual brand programs within Impact may have their own approval (some are pre-qualified, meaning you're auto-approved).

**Key brands available:** Canva, Semrush, Hostinger, NordVPN, Grammarly, and 4,000+ others. Many SaaS tools run their affiliate programs through Impact.

**Commission:** Varies by brand. Many SaaS programs offer 20-40% recurring.

**Why apply:** Single platform gives access to hundreds of SaaS affiliate programs. This is where you'll find programs for many of the tools you review.

---

## 3. ShareASale

**Signup URL:** https://www.shareasale.com/newsignup.cfm

**What you need:**
- Website URL (workmultiple.com)
- Brief site description
- Primary promotional method (content/SEO)
- Payment info (for $50 minimum payouts)

**Approval timeline:** 7-10 business days for compliance review. They may email for more info.

**Key brands available:** Many SMB-focused SaaS tools, WordPress plugins, email marketing tools, and more. Some overlap with Impact but different merchant mix.

**Commission:** Varies by merchant. Typical SaaS: 20-30% per sale.

**Why apply:** Complements Impact.com with a different set of merchants. Free to join.

---

## Recommended Order

1. **Amazon Associates** (fastest approval, start earning immediately on any product links)
2. **Impact.com** (most SaaS programs, pre-qualified brands available right away)
3. **ShareASale** (7-10 day wait, apply and forget)

## After Approval

Once approved, update the `AFFILIATE_LINK_PLACEHOLDER` URLs in all content engine drafts with real tracking links. Each article has 2-3 placeholder CTAs ready to be swapped.
